Pay range

The hourly wage range for this position is $15.00 – $18.50 per hour. The actual hourly rate will equal or exceed the required minimum wage applicable to the job location.

Additional compensation in the form of premiums may be paid in amounts ranging from $0.35 per hour to $3.00 per hour in specific circumstances. Premiums may be based on but not limited to: schedule, shift, working conditions, or geographic location.

Position Summary

As a Cashier / Front End Associate at Walmart, you greet customers, ring up purchases efficiently and accurately, handle returns, and keep the front-end area clean and stocked. You are the last face our customers see before they leave the store, so your friendly, can-do attitude sets the tone for the entire shopping experience.

What You'll Do

  • Greet customers and assist them through the checkout process.
  • Scan items, process payments (cash, card, EBT, mobile), and bag merchandise.
  • Handle returns, exchanges, and price checks per Walmart policy.
  • Maintain a clean, safe, and well-stocked front-end area.
  • Partner with the front-end coach to support a positive customer experience.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Must be 16 years of age or older.
  • Must be legally permitted to work in the United States.
  • Ability to lift up to 25 lbs and stand for extended periods.

Benefits

Eligible associates receive a comprehensive benefits package including paid time off, 401(k) match, associate stock purchase plan, education benefits through Live Better U, and merchandise discounts at Walmart and Sam's Club.
