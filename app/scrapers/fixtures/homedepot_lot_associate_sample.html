Position Purpose

Lot Associates assist customers with the loading of their vehicles and the return of merchandise to its proper place inside the store or garden area. Lot Associates also are responsible for the maintenance and cleanliness of the parking lot, the retrieval of shopping carts, and the safe operation of motorized equipment such as forklifts and yard trucks.

Each associate has the responsibility of providing a safe shopping environment by following all safety policies & standards (e.g., use of personal protective equipment, removing hazards), taking immediate action to resolve any hazards encountered, reporting unresolved issues, and ensuring Hazardous Materials are handled appropriately.

Key Responsibilities

  • Greet customers and assist with loading purchases into vehicles.
  • Retrieve shopping carts from the parking lot and return them to the store.
  • Maintain a clean, safe, and organized parking lot and garden area.
  • Operate motorized equipment such as forklifts and yard trucks in compliance with safety standards.
  • Partner with the store team to support a positive customer experience.

Minimum Qualifications

  • Must be 18 years of age or older.
  • Must be legally permitted to work in the United States.
  • Ability to work a flexible schedule, including evenings and weekends.
