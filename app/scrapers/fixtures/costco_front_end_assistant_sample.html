Job Summary

Front End Assistants help maintain the front-end of our warehouse, bag member purchases, retrieve and stage shopping carts from the parking lot, and provide friendly, efficient member service. This role is part of Costco's commitment to taking care of our employees and providing industry-leading wages and benefits.

Responsibilities

  • Bag member purchases at the registers efficiently and carefully.
  • Retrieve and stage shopping carts from the parking lot.
  • Stock and merchandise products in the front-end area.
  • Provide a high level of member service and assist with carry-outs.
  • Follow all safety policies, including safe lifting practices.

Benefits

Costco offers a comprehensive benefits package including health insurance, dental and vision coverage, a 401(k) plan with company match, paid time off, and an annual Costco Shop Card for eligible employees. Costco employees are paid weekly.

Required Qualifications

  • Must be 18 years of age or older.
  • Must be authorized to work in the United States.
  • Ability to work a flexible schedule including evenings, weekends, and holidays.
