Methodology & sources

How the gap is computed

For each active yard, competitor postings within 25 miles are blended via inverse-distance weighting (Σ wagei/di ÷ Σ 1/di). The gap is the blended competitive wage minus the ACME wage at that yard.

Cost-of-living adjustment

BEA Regional Price Parity normalizes wages by state. Adjusted figures are shown on each yard's detail page.

Data sources
  • Direct employer career sites ({{ benchmarks | length }} competitors)
  • BLS OEWS state-level baseline
  • BEA Regional Price Parity (state)
  • HUD USPS ZIP → CBSA crosswalk
  • Census Bureau Geocoder (lat/lng)
  • LLM extraction via OpenRouter
